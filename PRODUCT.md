# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective clients, collaborators, and employers visiting Mahdi's portfolio to judge taste, quality, and fit.

## Product Purpose

Present the work clearly, signal a calm and capable point of view, and convert interest into contact.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, editorial, precise, confident.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS landing pages, noisy dashboard cards, neon gradients, overbuilt template portfolios, and anything that feels like an AI-generated composition without a point of view.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the work, not with decoration.
- Keep hierarchy calm and readable, with clear editorial pacing.
- Use restraint to make the strong parts feel stronger.
- Let imagery, typography, and spacing carry the impression.
- Make contact easy without turning the page into a sales pitch.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, respect reduced-motion preferences, keep keyboard focus obvious, and ensure layouts remain usable and readable across viewport sizes.
